About

The Office of Guidance and Counseling at Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Sta. Mesa, Manila is dedicated to supporting the academic, personal, and professional growth of students through a variety of services including academic advising, career counseling, and personal counseling. It offers workshops, peer mentoring, and a resource library to enhance students' development and success, while promoting a respectful, inclusive environment.
